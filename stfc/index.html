Rules of Engagement (RoE)

"No Loot = Don't Shoot!"

Loot Box Empty :-(


What does this mean? When it comes to Protected Cargo, you are either Over or Under. When a ship is mining on a node, click on them and click Scan, you will see a box at the bottom of the window. Click it. If there is nothing in the left side, you may not attack them. This is known as UPC: Under Protected Cargo.

Loot Box Has Shit! :-)


If the box shows contents on the left, then it's known as Over Protected Cargo, or OPC. This is within RoE and you can blow their asses up.


Common Terms Used

You will see and hear all kinds of acronyms. Below are some of the most common ones.

  • RoE | Rules of Engagment
  • UPC | Under Protected Cargo
  • OPC | Over Protected Cargo

Battleships

The battle triangle shows you which ships are best for fighting which ships. Is this written in stone? No. Virtually any ship that is strong enough and has a good enough crew can take on just about any other ship. Just about. Not all.

Battleship triangle:

  • Explorers against Interceptors
  • Interceptors against Battleships
  • Battleships against Explorers
